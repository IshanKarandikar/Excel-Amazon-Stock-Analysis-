# 📊 Amazon Stock Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes **28 years of Amazon stock data (1997–2025)** using Microsoft Excel to uncover price trends, trading volume patterns, and yearly growth insights. The analysis is presented through an interactive dashboard with dynamic filters enabling drill-down by year, month, and day of week.

---

## Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel** — PivotTables, Charts, Dashboard Design
- **GitHub** — Version control and project hosting

---

## Dataset
- **Source:** Amazon Stock Data (AMZN) 1997–2025
- **Columns:** Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Adjusted Close, Volume

## Key Insights

- 📈 **Yearly Trends** — Amazon stock grew consistently from under $1 in 1997 to over $200 in 2025
- 📅 **Best Month** — August shows the highest average adjusted close price
- 📆 **Day of Week** — Trading volume is relatively consistent across weekdays
- 💰 **Average Close Price** — $43.99 across the full dataset
- 🔝 **Peak Year** — 2021 recorded the highest average adjusted close price
- 📉 **2022 Dip** — Significant price correction visible post-2021 peak

---

## Dashboard Features

- **KPI Cards** — Average High, Low, Open, and Close prices
- **Yearly Trends Chart** — Line chart showing adjusted close price from 1997–2025
- **Trading Volume Analysis** — Bar chart showing volume by month
- **High vs Low by Day** — Bar chart comparing price range across weekdays
- **Value Over Time** — Pie chart showing volume distribution by day
- **Open vs Close** — Bar chart comparing opening and closing prices by day
- **Dynamic Filters** — Slicers for Year, Month, and Day of Week
